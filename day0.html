All attendees have agreed to and are expected to follow the SSSC Code of Conduct at all times. A copy of the Mini-workshop Code of Conduct can be found here.

What is Wonder(Previously called YoTribe)?

Wonder (formerly Yotribe) is a virtual coffee hour. You can enter the Wonder room and join different circles for video chats or form your own circle. We'll be using this for our virtual coffee break before the start of the mini-workshop and for the topic group sessions. Feel free to pop in during breaks or other times in the mini-workshop when you want to have 1-on-1 conversations with attendees.
