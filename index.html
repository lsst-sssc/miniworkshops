Aims and Goals

A summary of survey strategy and cadence choices, evaluated by the Rubin Observatory LSST Scheduler Team, has been prepared for Rubin's Survey Cadence and Optimization Committee (SCOC). This report was released in August. In the coming months the SSSC is going to be asked to start giving feedback on these simulations. To help kick start this process and begin broad review of the cadence report results, we are organizing a 2-hour survey cadence mini-workshop spread out over 1-2 days in mid November. This workshop will include background information on cadence optimization and the operations simulations.
